How to Properly Saddle a Horse


There are multiple ways to saddle a horse. Everyone will do it differently and it is all based on prefrence, as well as the horse that is being saddled.

Main Parts to the Saddle

  • Front cinch
    • Back cinch
  • Breast collar
    • Latigo
  • Off-side Billet/Billet keeper
    • Front Rigging D Ring
  • Rear Rigging D Ring


How to Properly set the saddle up

Step One

First thing one should do when saddling a horse, is to brush the horse out. I always brush my horses out good before I saddle them uup and ride them.

Step Two

Step two is the put the saddle pad or blanket down on the horses back. The pad or blanket should not be too far forward or too far back on the horse. Improper placement can cause injury to the horse.

Step Three

The next step is to set the saddle down on the horses back and set it in the correct placement in correlation to the saddle pad or blanket.

Step Four

After you get your saddle in the correct spot, everything can be hooked up and connected the way it needs to be. Starting with the front cinch, then the back cinch, and lastly the breast collar.
