How to Saddle a Horse

Instructable

What you will need

  • Saddle Pad
    • Saddle
    • Bridle
      • Additional Things You May Need
        • Sport Boots
        • Bell Boots

Step One

Brush your horse out, especially where your saddle will sit.

Step Two: Put your saddle pad on, then your saddle on top of the pad.

Make sure your saddle is a good fit for your horse

  • Get the cinch and backcinch on the sides next to the stirups.
  • Make sure no straps or pieces on the saddle are stuck up under the saddle.

Step Three: Cinch up both of your cinches.

Do not leave your cinches loose, a horses leg can easily get caught

  • Tighten your front cinch first, that is what keeps your saddle on.
  • If you choose to ride with a back cinch, tighten it so it is snug to the horses stomach.

Step Four: Attach anything else on the saddle

When going around the saddle make sure everything is tight and nothing is loose.

  • Attach your breast collar.
  • Put on the bridle and attach a tie down if you choose to ride in one.
  • Put on the bell boots and sport boots.
